Features

Task Lists

Make different lists to keep yourself organized. If you need to separate tasks such as work and school you can do that with different lists.

Statistics

See statistics on past tasks/ chores that have been done such as average chores you do in a day or week. You can also see your family members points from chores and add or subtract points.

Add as Many Tasks You Want In A List

Why be so limited in things you can do when you can do them all. Add as many tasks you want, it is fully up to you.

Set Dates For Tasks And Or Lists

Add dates to lists and tasks as well as filter them by when you need to get them done. It is important that you are able to remember what you need to do and when to do it.

List People In Your Family

If you want to add people to do certain tasks add family members and add them to certain tasks and or lists.

Give Points To People In Your Family For Tasks/ Chores

Want to spice up your home chores? Add points for certain chores that family members can redeem for cash or a item such as an ice-cream cone.

Assign Tasks/ Chores To Family Members

When you have family members and you want to give them points, assign them to a task or list and add the amount of points they should get for that task or list.
