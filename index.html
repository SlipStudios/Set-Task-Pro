Welcome to Set Task Pro

Take control of your daily life with a powerful task and goal manager designed for both individuals and families. Whether you're tackling chores, setting goals, or managing multiple to-do lists, Set Task Pro has the tools to help you stay on track.

2x To-Do Lists: Organize your tasks into two customizable lists.

Set 1 Goal: Stay focused by setting and tracking one personal goal.

See Stats: Visualize your progress and productivity trends over time.

Set Dates and Times: Add deadlines or reminders to tasks for better scheduling.

Pro Plan

10+ To-Do Lists: Create multiple lists to manage different areas of life.

List Family Members: Add family members so you can assign tasks to them.

Make Chores & Give Points: Turn chores into fun point-based challenges to reward family members.

Assign Tasks or Lists: Share the workload by assigning specific tasks/lists to family members.

Set 10+ Goals: Stay motivated by tracking multiple personal or shared goals.
